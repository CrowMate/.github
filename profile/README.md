### Headless Hazard

Our flagship project currently in development.

**Genre:** Puzzle-Platformer with Second-Person Perspective
**Platforms:** PC (Steam)
**Engine:** Unity
**Status:** In Development

**Key Features:**
- Unique second-person perspective gameplay
- Solo narrative campaign with emotional storytelling
- Multiplayer party mode (2-8 players)
- Retro-futuristic 1980s-90s aesthetic
- Physics-based puzzle mechanics

**Description:**
*Headless Hazard* is a puzzle-platformer where you control a security robot whose head is permanently detached from its body. Navigate through a vast underground corporate complex by coordinating your head (camera) and body to solve increasingly complex environmental puzzles. Experience two distinct campaigns: a touching solo story about protecting a lost child, and a chaotic multiplayer mode where maintenance robots turn deadly test chambers into playgrounds.
